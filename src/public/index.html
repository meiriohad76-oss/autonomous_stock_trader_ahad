        Decision Flow

        How A Trade Reaches The List

        1. Fundamentals screen the universe Each tracked stock becomes eligible, watch, reject, or unscored before sentiment can promote it.
        2. Fresh evidence updates sentiment Only fresh, sourced market signals are allowed into decision-making. Seed/demo data is blocked in production mode.
        3. Selection Agent builds lists Buy, short/sell, and watch lists combine fundamentals, market regime, signals, risk, and runtime guardrails.
        4. Execution stays gated Alpaca paper orders require broker credentials, risk approval, and an explicit confirmation phrase.

        Selection Agent

        Combines Fundamentals, Market/Sector, and Signals workers into ranked buy, sell, watch, and blocked lists.

        1 Universe Screen

        News, SEC, insider, macro, and flow evidence must be fresh and sourced.

        3 Trade Lists

        The Selection Agent compiles buy, short/sell, and watch candidates.

        4 Risk + Alpaca

        Execution previews pass through broker readiness and Portfolio Risk before paper orders.
